About Public Tracking

How to use the cargo tracking interface

The public tracking interface allows you to track your cargo and receive information such as current status, estimated time of arrival, and detailed handling history.

  • Enter your tracking ID on the tracking page to see real-time cargo status.
  • No login is required — only your tracking ID is needed.
  • View your cargo's route on the interactive live map.
  • Browse the complete handling history from origin to destination.
Contact your booking agent if you don't know your tracking ID or need to modify your cargo.

Visit eclipse-ee4j.github.io/cargotracker for more information about Cargo Tracker.
